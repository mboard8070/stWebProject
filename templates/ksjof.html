Description


Kamodo Steve is an 2.5D action sidescroller where you play as Steve, an unwilling hero with unrealized power.

Steve and his BFF Shiloh team up to disrupt the sinister plans of the Robber Barons, a kabal of industrialists

whose only motivations are success, power and greed. They don't care who or what they have to hurt to get it.

Together, Steve and Shiloh use their unconventional skills to restore order to nature and their community.

...and explostions. There are a ridiculous amount of explosions.



Features


  • Explore large, post-industrial inspired landscapes with a surreal twist.
  • Run and gun, blast, or tactically overcome obstacles and enemies.
  • Expand your arsenal by collecting coins to purchase weapons.
  • Discover mutant creatures and save them.
  • Play solo or with a friend in local co-op mode.
  • Experience fast paced action in a side scroller with first person shooter style controls.
